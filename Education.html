EDUCATIONAL TOURS

Welcome to the Amuay Wildlife Park Education Center! We're thrilled you're interested in embarking on a journey of discovery with us. Amuay Wildlife Park isn't just a place to see animals it's a living classroom with opportunities to learn about the wonders of the natural world.

  • Our mission at Amuay Wildlife Park is all about Biodiversity and Conservation, and our Education Department provides unique opportunities to share this with the schools and groups.

  • Amuay Wildlife Park, a sprawling nature reserve nestled 350 km west of Venezuela's capital, is dedicated to protecting and showcasing the incredible biodiversity found within its diverse landscapes. Our Education Department plays a vital role in this mission by offering unique learning experiences for schools and groups who visit. Through immersive tours, interactive activities, and engaging programs, we ignite a passion for conservation and inspire lifelong appreciation for the natural world. Exploring over 72 square kilometers of grasslands, hills, and marshes, visitors encounter a breathtaking array of wildlife and plant life. We believe that education is the key to fostering responsible stewardship of the environment. Join us at Amuay Wildlife Park and embark on a journey of discovery, where learning meets adventure! This revised statement emphasizes the park's unique location, diverse ecosystems, and commitment to both biodiversity and education. It also highlights the range of educational experiences offered and the park's ultimate goal: inspiring future generations of conservationists.

  • CHALLENGING THE FUTURE: INSPIRING RESPONSIBLE ACTION

  • Our educational tours go beyond simple observation. We challenge participants to think critically about the environmental issues facing our planet. By fostering a deeper understanding of the delicate balance of ecosystems and the threats faced by wildlife, we empower future generations to become responsible stewards of the environment.
